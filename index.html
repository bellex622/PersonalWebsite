Hi! I am Belle. I grew up in Qingdao, a coastal city in China, and came to the United States for college. I completed my undergraduate and graduate degrees at SUNY Buffalo, where I studied accounting and auditing. After graduation, I did two internships in New York City for a year, then I returned to China and worked as a financial auditor. Over the past years, I spent about half of the year traveling for business, which also gave me many opportunities to explore the local attractions.

In 2020, I adopted a British Shorthair cat named Mianmian. He is a shy boy but loves to be close to me. He is a picky eater and absolutely adores snacks. Every time I take out a treat, he runs to me in a second. When I moved back to the United States, I also brought him along with me. I am currently living in Long Island New York, and by the end of June I will be moving to another city called Binghamton in upstate New York.
